I am your textbook definition of the word 'nerd'. As a lover of all things Mathematics and Computer Science, I get overly excited about my new calculators, I think about programs I could write to make my life easier, and could quite literally die of laughter from a good math or programming joke. I am currently in my final (hopefully) year of the Actuarial Science program at the University of the West Indies, Mona Campus and am hoping to become an actuary and a data analyst.

While web development is unrelated to my studies and career aspirations, I am not deterred from wanting to learn something new. Whenever I scroll the websites for tech companies like Apple and Samsung, I'm always amazed by how interactive and immersive the pages are. I almsot instinctively start to think about the programming behind the visual effects, and I hope to be exposed to the like in this course. My intention is not to start freelancing, but who knows what the future holds....
